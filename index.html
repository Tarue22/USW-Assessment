About

Growing up l always wanted to be a pilot but that was until l got my first ever computer. l discovered a whole new world, it was like a child who had discovered ice cream for the first time. Over time my interest and passion for computers grew and later on was when l decided to take up Computer Science. I would not say l am good but l am a work in progress, mostly l enjoy coding, designing as well as developing and I enjoy turning messy ideas into tidy interfaces. In my spare time l like to relax and watch some movies and documentaries, l also appreciate a good read fiction preferably. I like sports, back in highschool l used to be part of the rugby, swimming and tennis teams, l am not so invested in them anymore as l have now started University. I started at the University of South Wales in 2024 doing my foundation year where we were introduced to university life and were taught how to navigate it. I passed my foundation and progressed to first year and so far so good.
